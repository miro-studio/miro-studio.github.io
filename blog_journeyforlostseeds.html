Mr.Flower's Journey for lost Seeds - Development Log

It was the first level game that I made.

It's a tile map, each stages have 5 levels so in total there are 15 scenes.

So it took quite a while to finish this game compared to previous games.

If you water green sapling then it grows and becomes tall flower so you can step on it.

And red sapling grows into the insect-eating plant so you can get rid of bug and go somewhere that you couldn't go.

And you get stronger when you're soacked in water, but cloud will dry you.
